## Legal Terms — Confirmed Controlling Facts

All confirmed verbatim in the live TxODDS terms:

  "materially controlled" by a bot/agent may be disqualified and TxODDS may require identity
  verification of human involvement. (Superteam payload also has `agentAccess: HUMAN_ONLY`.)
- "Participants can enter multiple tracks but cannot win more than one prize in total."

  built during the hackathon.
- Judges must never need to pay fees, buy tokens/subscriptions, or create wallets/accounts;
  violating this is grounds for disqualification.

  undefined — see open questions). Do not commit recorded TxLINE payloads to the public

- Prize ops: paid to one nominated member, gas fees deducted, winner pays taxes, 30-day
  affidavit deadline or forfeits to alternate, USD 500 aggregate liability cap, contracting
  entity is TXODDS Services LLC (Illinois), English law.

## TxLINE Facts The Reference Files Missed (all verified in docs/spec)

- **Replay superpower:** `GET /api/odds/snapshot/{fixtureId}` and
  `GET /api/scores/snapshot/{fixtureId}` accept optional `asOf` (Unix ms) — point-in-time
  historical snapshots. Simplest possible demo-replay mechanism.
- `GET /api/scores/historical/{fixtureId}` only works for fixtures started **6 hours to
  2 weeks ago** — group-stage matches age out before the deadline; don't build replay on it.
- SSE streams accept `fixtureId` query filter and `Last-Event-ID` resume header.
- `GET /api/odds/validation` requires `messageId` + `ts` captured from the odds record —
  you must store them at ingestion time to fetch proofs later.
- Extra endpoints: `GET /api/fixtures/updates/{epochDay}/{hourOfDay}` (schedule changes),
  `GET /api/fixtures/validation`, `GET /api/fixtures/batch-validation` (fixture-level Merkle
  proofs), `POST /api/guest/purchase/quote` (paid tiers only). `fixtures/snapshot` takes
  `startEpochDay` for paging; `stat-validation` has a V2 `statKeys` mode.
- **No API rate limits** on the free tier; free tier permits commercial use and includes
  historical replay.
- **TxL mint is Token-2022** — legacy SPL ATA helpers will break on subscribe.
- Activation signature format: ed25519-detached over `${txSig}:${leagues.join(",")}:${jwt}`,
  base64-encoded. Guest JWT expires in ~30 days.
- Subscriptions are 4-week (28-day) multiples; free tiers renew at no cost. Anchor program
  is `txoracle`; PDA seeds `token_treasury_v2`, `pricing_matrix`; per-network IDLs at
  `/documentation/programs/{mainnet,devnet}`. Addresses page also lists USDT mints and
  per-network guest-auth URLs.
- On-chain `validate_stat` proofs cover **team-level stats only** (stat keys 1–8: goals,
  yellows, reds, corners per participant; period variant `(period*1000)+key`).

## Discrepancies Worth Knowing

- The Superteam listing text "open to individuals, teams, and AI agents" conflicts with the
  legal terms' natural-persons rule. Terms control: enter as humans.
- "Free through 2026-07-19 23:59 UTC" appears only in Superteam listing text; TxLINE docs
  describe renewable 28-day free subscriptions with no sunset date.
- Technical-documentation link is an **optional** form field despite the prose calling it
  required — provide it anyway.
- The trading-track checklist line "include recorded TxLINE samples if licensing permits"
  conflicts with the terms' redistribution ban — default to NOT committing data; ship a
  replay script that re-fetches with the runner's own credentials.
- superteam.fun intermittently serves mis-cached pages across track URLs (a trailing-slash
  URL returned the wrong track). Cache-bust and verify the payload slug when re-checking.
